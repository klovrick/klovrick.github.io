Corruption Heist

31:21-32:00

They find out that the NYPD use a special taxi service allowing criminals to be escorted by corrupt cops, which grant them freedom.

31:21-37:23

43:08-46:17

They fly to Los Angeles, where they fence the jewels to Redfoot, a friend of Mcmanus. In return, Redfoot provides them with a jewelry heist.

Scene #3

Jewelry Heist

46:19-50:38

46:19-46:23

This second heist involved stealing from another jeweler, Saul, which Redfoot assured would be an easy job for the men.

46:27-48:20

However, the heist ends badly; Saul and his bodyguards are all killed during the fight, despite the group obtaining the goods.

48:39-49:00

When examining the briefcase the group stole, they discover that the "goods" were not jewels, but drugs.

50:14-50:29

They become thieves and murderers from this heist, and are blackmailed by Redfoot.

49:16-50:35

It is revealed that the job was given to Redfoot by a man named Kobayashi, who wanted to meet with them.

Scene #4

Keyser Sozé

51:56-59:12/1:05:10-1:07:03/1:07:36-1:13:07

51:56-53:35

The meeting with Kobayashi takes place. He introduces himself as a lawyer working for Keyser Sozé, a Turkish crimelord, blackmails them into working for Sozé as well.

53:35-56:05

All of them are handed files, where information about their entire life has been recorded and tracked. It turns out that each man had stolen from Sozé at one point in their life. They are all indebted to him.

56:05-59:11

A new heist is offered: to raid an Argentenian ship selling $91 million worth of drugs, in return for cash and freedom from their debt.

1:05:11-1:07:00

The next morning, the group finds that Fenster ran away to avoid doing the heist. However, after some time, they find his dead body - the consequence of his cowardice.

1::07:36-1:13:07

The remaining men corner Kobayashi and argue with him, but he threatens them with violence and the safety of their loved ones.

Scene #5

One Last Heist

1:13:08-1:22:24/1:23:57-1:30:01/1:31:20-1:37:41

1:13:08-1:26:48

The raid starts, and they kill most of the men on board. They are unaware that Keyser Sozé is onboard the ship as well.

1:26:49-1:28:25

As the raid progresses, it is uncovered that there were no drugs on the ship at all - it was all a hoax created by Sozé.

1:29:02-1:31:50

When the group was split up, Sozé kills all of them one by one, and sets the ship on fire.

1:32:41-1:36:31

There are two survivors left - Verbal Kint and Arkosh Kavash, an Argentenian man from the boat. At the detective's office, Verbal Kint gives his testimony, and refuses to believe that Sozé is Dean Keaton, no matter how much the detective, Kujan, tries to convince him.

1:36:31-1:37:39

Verbal Kint decides to trust that he witnessed Sozé kill Keaton, and insists on posting bail and leaving, risking the danger of Sozé finding and eliminating him as well.
